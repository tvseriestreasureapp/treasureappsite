Treasure App Tv Series

A web series / television show, centered on individuals in Ghana who come into possession of a mysterious app; An app that can grant wishes within certain constructs with restrictions. These characters, some of whom are in different places and situations find themselves thrust in this game of cat and mouse as they all use the app per their motives and for their own gain.

Created by Kofi S. Acquah & Sally Polley, the series explores the concept of morality and desires. What would one do in a certain situation if given the opportunity to get anything in the world?.
